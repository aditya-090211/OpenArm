# Changelog

All notable changes and development progress for OpenArm will be documented in this file.

---

# v0.1.0 — Initial Concept Development
## November 2023

### Added
- First robotic arm prototype using:
  - candy sticks
  - cardboard
  - micro servos
  - Arduino
- Basic arm motion testing
- Early gripper experimentation
- Initial linkage and joint concepts

### Notes
This stage focused primarily on learning:
- servo control
- arm movement
- mechanical linkages
- basic robotics concepts

---

# v0.2.0 — Structural Experimentation
## 2024

### Added
- Larger prototype concepts
- PVC-based arm structures
- Higher torque servo experimentation
- Improved arm geometry concepts
- Early modularity exploration

### Improved
- Stability
- Range of motion
- Joint durability

### Notes
Development began shifting from simple prototypes toward more practical and scalable robotic arm systems.

---

# v0.3.0 — CAD Development Phase
## 2025

### Added
- Transition to CAD-based development using Onshape
- Multiple robotic arm CAD prototypes
- Gear-driven joint concepts
- Improved structural designs
- Multi-axis arm concepts
- Advanced gripper prototypes

### Improved
- Structural rigidity
- Mechanical layout
- Manufacturability
- Design iteration speed

### Notes
This stage marked the transition from experimental physical prototypes to more advanced engineering-focused development.
